## Key Features

- **Native Bitcoin Payments**: No payment processors needed
- **Instant Settlement**: Direct peer-to-peer transactions
- **Low Fees**: Minimal transaction costs
- **Global Reach**: Accept payments from anywhere
- **Smart Contracts**: Automated escrow and dispute resolution

## Use Cases

- Digital goods marketplaces
- Service platforms
- NFT marketplaces
- Peer-to-peer trading
- Subscription services
